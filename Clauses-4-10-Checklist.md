# ISO/IEC 27001:2022 Clauses 4-10 Audit Checklist

**Reference:** ISO/IEC 27001:2022 Management System Clauses
**Prepared by:** TrustGrid Technology

---

## How to Use This Checklist

For each requirement, verify both that documentation exists AND that it reflects actual practice. A policy that exists but isn't followed is a nonconformity just as much as a missing policy.

**Status Key:** Conforms / Nonconformity / Observation / Not Applicable
