# ISO/IEC 27001:2022 Annex A Controls Checklist

**Reference:** ISO/IEC 27001:2022 Annex A (93 controls across 4 themes)
**Prepared by:** TrustGrid Technology

---

## How to Use This Checklist

Before auditing any control, check the organisation's Statement of Applicability (SoA) to confirm whether it is marked applicable. Do not audit controls the organisation has justified as not applicable, but do verify the justification itself is reasonable.

**Status Key:** Conforms / Nonconformity / Observation / Not Applicable
